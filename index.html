My name is Ben

I am an engineer. My background is in CPU design and hardware verification. I currently work as a Researcher at the University of Bristol. I develop ways of building CPUs to securely and efficiently execute cryptographic workloads, and study how to defend them against side-channel attacks.

You can find me on Github, LinkedIn (if you must) and Twitter (very rarely).

Projects:

  • uc64. A 64-bit RISC-V Micro-controller CPU
  • I'm also helping build and edit the RISC-V Instruction Set Extension for Cryptography.
